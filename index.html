Travel to Osaka

Osaka, the Nation's Kitchen

While being the third most populated city in Japan, it's one often overlooked by travelers. While not rich in dazzling architecture or beautiful natural scenery like other popular Japanese cities, it has something different to offer. It's a foodie's paradise with an urban feel but a small-town charm.

Try any of Osaka's specialty dishes, such as okonomiyaki, kistune udon, and takoyaki. Then stop over to Dotonburi and Shinsekai for an old retro town vibe saturated in the Japanese identity. Shop 'til you drop at its many amazing shopping districts in Nipponbashi, Shinsaibashi, Namba, and Umeda. Osaka has something to offer to any traveler. If you want the Tokyo experience but with a warmer hospitality and more richness in culture, then take a trip to Osaka!
